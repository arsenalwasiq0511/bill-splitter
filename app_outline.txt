Bill Splitter Outline:

**start from perspective of a restaurant bill**
**where one of the customers inputs into the app**

User Actions
- input each item on a bill
  - name of food
  - cost of food
  - way to share cost of an item amongst ppl
- input names of ppl in the group
  - and number of people
  * maybe link to venmo/fb/phone
- add tax/tip
- return itemised bill per person

Additional Features
* link to venmo - create venmo payment  
* input picture of receipt

Page Views/Functions
- home page
- add number/names of people
- add food item
- split bill
- link to venmo
